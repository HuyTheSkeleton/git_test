Iris

Iris (Japanese: アイリス Iris) is a Dragon-type Trainer.

She was one of the Gym Leaders of Opelucid City's Gym, known officially as the Opelucid Gym, giving out the Legend Badge, in Pokémon White.

Two years later, in Pokémon Black 2 and White 2, she is the Champion of the Unova region's Pokémon League.
